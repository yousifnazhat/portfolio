# Blueprint

## Overview

This is a portfolio website designed to showcase projects and skills. It features a modern, single-page layout with smooth scrolling navigation.

- Add fade-in animations on scroll to the different sections of the page.

## Design and Features

### General
- Single-page layout
- Smooth scrolling navigation
- Responsive design for mobile and web

### Sections
- **Hero Section:** A prominent section at the top of the page to grab the user's attention.
- **About Me Section:** A dedicated section that provides a brief introduction about the owner of the portfolio, their skills, and their passions. The section is designed to be visually appealing and easy to read, with a clear heading and a few paragraphs of text. It also includes a list of skills or areas of expertise, which are highlighted with a distinct style to draw the user's attention. The content focuses on the owner's role as a student and leader, their passion for the intersection of hardware and security, and their love for their Shih Tzu puppy.
- **Projects Section:** A section to showcase a list of projects.
- **Email Section:** A contact form to allow users to send emails.
- **Footer:** A footer with relevant links and information.

### Styling
- The project uses Tailwind CSS for styling.
- It has a modern and clean design aesthetic.
